Admission Requirements for HCIT

  • A national senior certificate with an endorsement for addmission to a higher certificate and/ or diploma and/or degree; or
  • A national certificate vocational (NVC) at NFQ level 4 within an associted field and levels of achievements or:
  • Mature age exemption for students above the of 23 as set out in the higer education regulations; or
  • Recognition of prior learning (RPL)
